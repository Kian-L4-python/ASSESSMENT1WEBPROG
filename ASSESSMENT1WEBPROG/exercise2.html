Plot

Barry the Bee, who just graduated from college, is uninspired by the idea of working with honey. He breaks a fundamental principle of his species by speaking to a human and flying outside the hive for the first time. Barry discovers that people have been stealing and consuming honey for ages and decides that his ultimate calling is to bring about justice for his species by accusing people of stealing.
